What is Hollow Knight?

Hollow Knight is a metroidvania style game that was released in 2017 which was developed and published by Australia Independent Team Cherry. In the game the player controls a nameless Insectoid or Buglike character, explore the kingdom of Hallownest, which is a fallen kingdom that is plauged by a supernatural mind controlling disease created by an ancient God known as The radiance. The Kingdom is maze like ad the palyer can get lost for hours exploring the secrets that it has to offer.

Hornet is the daughter of the Pale King and Herrah the Beast, the queen of Deepnest. Her birth was the result of a bargain for her mother to become a Dreamer, Her shared father with the Knight and the rest of the Vessels makes them half-siblings.

The White Lady is a root higher being who used to be the Queen of Hallownest and the wife of the Pale King. The White Lady is the mother of the Vessels. Out of shame for her part in their creation, she bound herself in the Queen's Gardens to stop herself from having more offspring.
