What is Hollow Knight?

Hollow Knight is a metroidvania style game that was released in 2017 which was developed and published by Australia Independent Team Cherry. In the game the player controls a nameless Insectoid or Buglike character, explore the kingdom of Hallownest, which is a fallen kingdom that is plauged by a supernatural mind controlling disease created by an ancient God known as The radiance. The Kingdom is maze like ad the palyer can get lost for hours exploring the secrets that it has to offer.

The Pale King is a higher being who was the monarahc of Hallownest, mate to the White Lady and ruler of the White palace, was an ancient Wyrm traveling through mountains until arriving in Kingdoms Edge, hatching and taking his form as The Pale King

The Pure Vessel was the original Hollow Knight, a vessel created by the Pale King to contain the Radiance and its Infection. Trained and raised in the White Palace to become a perfect, vessel with no mind or will to be corrupted, eventually failing because of an attachment to its father, the Pale King

An ancient, higher being of light, worshipped by the Moth Tribe until the Pale King granted sentience to his subjects, leading them to abandon her and causing her to become forgotten. The Infection is her revenge, spreads through dreams, stripping bugs of their minds

Hornet is the daughter of the Pale King and Herrah the Beast, the queen of Deepnest. Her birth was the result of a bargain for her mother to become a Dreamer, Her shared father with the Knight and the rest of the Vessels makes them half-siblings.

The White Lady is a root higher being who used to be the Queen of Hallownest and the wife of the Pale King. The White Lady is the mother of the Vessels. Out of shame for her part in their creation, she bound herself in the Queen's Gardens to stop herself from having more offspring.

Hollow Knights first major update, added fresh content to expand the world. Added Grey Prince Zote and White Defender bosses, and Dream Gate ability. Expanded the game on its idea of dreams

The Grimm Troupe DLC adds a dark, circus-themed storyline to Hollow Knight, revolving around a mysterious group led by Troupe Master Grimm. Added Troupe Master Grimm boss and several new Charms

The Lifeblood DLC serves as a smaller update compared to the other major expansions, but introduces several quality of life changes. It focuses on improving gameplay

Godmaster is one of the most substantial DLCS. Adds GodHome where you can challenge the Gods of hallownest. Also adds Pantheons which act basically as boss rushes in the game and also a new ending to the game.

The Pale Court is a Fan-Made dlc that introduces several new boss fights. It allows you to fight the five great knights of Hallownest which ere the Pale Kings loyal servants, also adds Call of the Champion
